This is my school (Govt. Gorkhi H.S. School)

The city of literature and music, Gwalior, which was the tapobhoomi of Rishi Galav, is also famous for its educational and cultural institutions. Government Gorkhi Higher Secondary School located near Jiaji Chowk (Maharaj Bada), the heart of Gwalior, is more than a century old (establishment year 1903-04) From) has a proud history. The students of this school have given meaningful Guru-Dakshina to the dedicated teachers of the school by hoisting their fame-ensign not only in the state or the nation, but all over the world. It is worth mentioning here that Bharat Ratna, the three-time Prime Minister of India, Shri Atal Bihari Bajpayee's son-in-law of Mother-Bharati, has been a successful student of this Gorkhi. Since the beginning of the last 20th century, the school spreads its education, even in the 21st century and is vigorously engrossed in spiritual practice like a yogi, a seeker, in the work of building excellent citizens for the service of Mother India. Be it medical field or engineering, politics or education field, administrative field or literature, social field or spirituality, the students who have studied from here have agitated the society with their aura and talent.
